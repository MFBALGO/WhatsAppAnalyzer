Upload Your WhatsApp Chat



How to Export Your WhatsApp Chat:

For iPhone Users:

  1. Open the individual or group chat you want to save from your WhatsApp.
  2. Tap on the chat name at the top to open chat details.
  3. Scroll down and tap on "Export Chat".
  4. Choose to not include media files.
  5. Select WhatsApp or another app to send the chat to.
  6. Download and extract the chat file as .txt and upload it here.

For Android Users:

  1. Open the individual or group chat you want to save from your WhatsApp.
  2. Tap on the three dots in the top right corner > More > Export chat.
  3. Choose to not include media files.
  4. Select WhatsApp or another app to send the chat to.
  5. Download and extract the chat file as .txt and upload it here.
